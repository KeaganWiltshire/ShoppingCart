About this project

The purpose of this project

This project was first proposed by Rothman to help schools keep track of records at sports days.
Due to the complexity of having to gather a multitude of records from a vast amount of score keepers whose handwriting might not always be legible, we have created a web-based application to help ease the burden of collection and allow one to more easily compare current records with a student's previous records.
